Read:Advent

Read the Bible in 26 days!

Advent is a season in which we tell each other the redemptive story of Jesus as a way of looking forward to his future coming. One of the best ways to experience this story is through the actual words of the Scriptures.

This reading plan will take you through the Bible, mostly in order, from the first Sunday of Advent (November 29) through Christmas Eve. On Christmas Eve you will read the Hallelujah Psalms, God's discourse with Job, the final chapters of 2 Chronicles (the traditional ending of the Hebrew Scriptures), and Revelation.

Taken together, each day's reading will take 3-4 hours, but we recommend splitting it up by major reading section (Psalms, Torah/Former Prophets, Latter Prophets/Writings, New Testament) over the course of the day. This will be quite a time commitment, but imagine the joy of reading the entirety of Scripture just before we celebrate the birth of our King.
